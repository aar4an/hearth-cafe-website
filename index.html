Our Story

More than coffee —
a place to belong

The Hearth Café was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves a spot that feels like a second home. A place where the barista knows your order, the seats are always warm, and time slows down.

We source our beans directly from small farms in Coorg and Chikmagalur, roasted in small batches to honour each harvest. Nothing here is rushed.

🌱

Farm-Direct Sourcing

Every bean we use is traceable to a named farm and farmer.

🤝

Community First

We host open-mic nights, art shows, and slow Sunday sessions.

♻️

Zero-Waste Kitchen

Compostable packaging, spent-grounds garden, no single-use plastic.
